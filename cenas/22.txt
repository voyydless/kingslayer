He strikes first, you’re too slow.
Maybe it's the exhaustion. Maybe you're more shaken than expected from finding that
note. He throws a knife at you, plunging into your ribs, leaving your breath in a
choking gasp. You stumble back, but he’s already on you.
A boot to your chest sends you sprawling.
Your weapon clatters from your grasp.

"You're reputation had me thinking you'd give me a good fight." You hear him say,
staring down at you as your vison fades to black.

Hours later, you wake in chains, slumped over horseback.
The road was paved, and you saw golden gates behind you. Maybe losing a bit of
your dignity was worth it— you just got a free ride to your final destination.
